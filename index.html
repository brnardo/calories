    Profile

    Your daily targets are calculated from this profile (Mifflin-St Jeor × activity, goal pace applied, protein from body weight, fat at 25% of calories, carbs fill the rest, fiber at 14g / 1000 kcal).

    Target = rate × body weight. On Auto the rate follows your goal, because dieting needs more protein to preserve muscle: lose 0.5 kg/wk → 2.2 g/kg · lose 0.25 → 2.0 · lose 0.15 → 1.8 · maintain → 1.6 · gain → 1.8. Based on ISSN guidance (1.4–2.0 g/kg for active people), Morton 2018 (muscle-building plateau ≈1.6 g/kg) and Helms et al. (higher needs in a deficit).
